# Biography

I am a biochemistry and computer science major at Columbia University, driven by a passion to learn and expand my knowledge, which makes me a hard worker and a quick and effective learner.

I am especially passionate about learning the underlying molecular mechanisms in cancer and immunology, and the potential for engineering these systems to impact human health.

My interests extend into computer science, especially machine learning and AI. I hope to use these as research tools to help elucidate and target disease pathways.

I am a strong believer in service and civic engagement, and seek to make a positive societal impact in whatever project I choose.
